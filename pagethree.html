Go Back?

I remember the first time I got a laptop in my household. My mom was going back to school and we had to get the Internet by holding a yellow ethernet cable in the laptop.

Being the older child in my house meant I could use the laptop more, but acess to the sites I used now, let's just say was very limited.

It took us a long time to actually get wireless Internet without going to my grandpa's house, going on YouTube without supervision.
